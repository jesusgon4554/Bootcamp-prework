Bio

The Four Seasons is a rock band composed in the 1960's by lead singer Franki Valli originated in Newmark, New Jersey. The four man group have made many hit songs which became popular and influencial such as Can't Take My Eyes Off of You, Sherry, Walk like a Man, and Big Girls Don't Cry. They released their first album in 1961 and continued to inspire the music industy up until their break up in 1977 when their music was no longer popular due to the movement into hard rock.
